Recipe Book

Breakfast • Appetizers • Side Dishes • Sandwiches • Entrées • Desserts

 

For Christmas in 2021, I made my girlfriend, Cindy, a jar filled with over 200 recipe ideas with the idea being we would pull an item, find a recipe online, go to the store for the ingredients, and then cook the meal. I also bought an empty cookbook for us to then record our journey -- to save what we liked and comment on what we would have done differently for next time. As I reworked this website, I quickly realized the opportunity to preserve our journey online and to use it as a way to save recipes and to quickly share them with others. So, if for some reason you've stumbled upon this page, welcome! I hope that you enjoy the recipes we've collected and put our own twist on. If you try any of them out, shoot me an email! I'd love to hear from you.

- Derek
