Guacamole
Author: Derek Nichols | Cooked: March 26, 2022

Yields: 6 Servings | Prep Time: 10 Minutes | Cook Time: 5 Minutes

Ingredients
  • 3 avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 c. diced red onion
  • 3 Tbs chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
Directions
  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. For best results, refrigerate 1 hour before serving.
Additional Notes
  • White onion also works well.
